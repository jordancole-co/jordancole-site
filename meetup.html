Using meditation to make real changes in your everyday life.

Active Awareness is about paying attention to what's going on inside of you — your thoughts, your emotions — and using that info to guide actions in your everyday life.

It combines a simple style of meditation with a few reflection questions, connecting how you currently feel with something you can do about it.

Each week I host a free 30-minute Zoom call where we do this practice together.
