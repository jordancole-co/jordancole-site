1-on-1 Coaching

Go deeper with guidance.

The free practice works on its own. But if you want someone in your corner — helping you see your own patterns, stay accountable, and move through the stuff that feels stuck — that's what coaching is for. Everything I do is built on four core principles:

01
It's already within you.
The peace of mind we chase through external accomplishments can only ever be found internally.
02
Meditation is simply a tool.
It's the best tool we have to reconnect to our own inner wisdom. Sitting with what's happening inside provides the insight that shifts how we engage with life.
03
Action matters just as much.
Meditation builds the connection. Taking action based on what you learn from it is what creates the real change in your everyday life.
04
Enjoy the ride.
Life isn't a burden we have to carry — it's a game we get to play. The game isn't about reaching some future goal, it's about enjoying the step you're taking right now.

About
