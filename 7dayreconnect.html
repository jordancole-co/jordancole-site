And after all this time, I've learned that the "keep doing" part is the most important.

The 7 Day Reconnect

  • Day 1 — A 45-60 minute Zoom call to kick things off. We meditate together, journal, and talk through the experience.
  • Days 2-3 — Use a 15-minute guided audio meditation. WhatsApp check-ins: you send me what you're noticing, I make sure you're showing up.
  • Day 4 — A 30-minute phone call mid-week to see how it's going.
  • Days 5-6 — Use the guided audio recording for 15 minutes both days. We're still using WhatsApp for checking in: I make sure you show up, you send me what you're noticing and questions.
  • Day 7 — A final 45-60 minute Zoom call to close out the week. We meditate, journal, and talk through what shifted.

You'll have a week's worth of real observations about your patterns that normally go unnoticed.

And you'll understand how powerful the simple act of showing up for yourself can be.

Nothing fancy. No frills. Just taking the time to sit down and remember what it's like to be cool with just being you.
